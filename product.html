Why Intelligent Logistics

Your logistics data is everywhere. We bring it together.

Centralize and normalize multi-carrier data

Most organizations struggle with fragmented carrier data, manual reporting, limited data retention, and reactive issue management. Intelligent Logistics centralizes and normalizes all carrier tracking and billing data into one powerful analytics platform.

  • Identify and eliminate wasteful shipping charges
  • Automate operational and financial reporting
  • Proactively manage delivery issues before customers call
  • Retain proof-of-delivery and shipment records indefinitely
  • Analyze carrier performance across your entire network
Core Capabilities

Multi-Carrier. Normalized. Real-Time. Intelligent Logistics ingests data from all major parcel, LTL, and freight carriers — including UPS®, FedEx, DHL, USPS, Old Dominion, and more — and normalizes it into a single, easy-to-use application.

  • Cloud-based analytics (no on-prem infrastructure)
  • Real-time tracking and event monitoring
  • Carrier-agnostic reporting
  • Indefinite data and POD retention
  • SFTP and BI tool integrations
  • Unlimited users with role-based permission
